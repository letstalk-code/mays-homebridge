## 1. PROJECT SCOPE & DELIVERABLES
The Developer agrees to design, build, and launch a custom 5-page website for the Client, which includes the following features and integrations:

*   **Custom 5-Page Website:** Professionally designed pages including Home, Services, About, Testimonials, and Contact (or similar structure as agreed).
*   **Booking Consultation System:** Fully integrated calendar for clients to book strategy sessions directly on the site.
*   **Zoom Integration:** Automated Zoom link generation for scheduled calls.
*   **Payment Processing:** Secure existing on-site payment capability for client bookings.
*   **Email Automation:** Automated confirmation emails and follow-up sequences for new bookings.

## 3. CANCELLATION & OWNERSHIP TERMS

The Client is free to cancel this agreement at any time. Ownership of the website and release of files are subject to the following timeline:

### 3.2. Cancellation After 6 Months
