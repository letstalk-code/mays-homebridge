2. How to Invite Us

  1. Log into your Stripe Dashboard at dashboard.stripe.com.
  2. In the top right, click the Settings (Gear Icon).
  3. Under the "Business Settings" column, click Team.
  4. Click + New Member in the top right.
  5. Enter our agency email: [YOUR EMAIL]
  6. Select the Developer role.
  7. Click Send Invite.
